Educational Opportunity Program

The Educational Opportunity Program also known as EOP, is a program dedicated to students who are considered low income, first generation college students, or historically underrepresented. Itâ€™s purpose is to assist students in balancing personal obligations with the academic demands of UC Berkeley Students, selecting courses, deciding on a major, understanding financial aid, exploring career options, and preparing for life after Berkeley. EOP also assists dealing with the various extenuating circumstances all while ensuring that they continue on their path to graduation. Students can access counselors through same day appointments virtually. You can also pre-schedule an appointment up to 2 weeks in advance.
