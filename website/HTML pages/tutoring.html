Tutoring

For tutoring resources students are able to access the Student Learning Center, also known as SLC. The Student Learning Center's purpose is to help students reach their full academic potential by providing tutoring services for a variety of different subjects. SLC offers a hybrid drop-in tutoring for all students. Another tutoring resource is Berkeley Connect. This is a program where they offer undergraduates their own personal graduate student mentor, connects you with other students who share your academic interests, and assists you in developing connections with professors and alumni. In order to get involved you must enroll in a Berkeley Connect section when registration opens for the semester. There are no prerequisites to take a Berkeley Connect class.
