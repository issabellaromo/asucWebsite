Mental Health

All the resources included here are related to mental health. Learn some self-care tips as we all face challenges during a pandemic. Additionally, if you are in need of support during your time here at Cal, there are many counseling, social nutrition, and psychological services available. Whether or not it be an emergency, we offer in-person and group counseling, so please do not hesitate to use these resources because everyone deserves healing.
