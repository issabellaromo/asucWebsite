Technology

The Student Tech Services also known as STS, is designed to ensure that all students at Berkeley have access to technological resources. This is a wide range consisting of device lending, tech support, remote access, and computer access to all campus libraries. These resources can help students that are facing issues with Wi-Fi connectivity and Zoom.

Technology Resources

Student Tech Services
What are Student Tech Services (STS)? Certain services are provided to all students using the Student Tech Fund, which failed to be renewed during the 2020 ASUC Elections cycle. Currently, STS provides a wide variety of services ranging from physical Device Lending and Remote Learning support to Berkeleytime, a website that hosts information on class statistics, and free software.
