Financial Aid

If you are a Berkeley student who receives financial aid and has a Federal Work-Study award, you can browse for on-campus work study employment through your student portal. You can use the career center to help you create resumes and cover letters for work study positions. Students are also able to access Student Legal Services. Their goal is to refer and guide students who are considering legal action, but they do not provide representation. Berkeley Students are also able to access the ASUC - Legal Clinic. Their mission is to provide free and confidential legal research tailored to clients' specific issues. Clients can drop in or make an appointment to address their legal concerns. The Clinic also participates in the Volunteer Income Tax Assistance (VITA) program, which provides free tax preparation services to qualified students and community members.
